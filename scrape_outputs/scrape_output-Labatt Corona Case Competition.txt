Labatt 2024 Corona Case Competition 2.0 Registration
We are excited to announce the second annual Corona Case Competition, an unparalleled case competition for students like you to showcase your analytical thinking, strategic planning, and presentation prowess. This event is designed to push the boundaries of your knowledge and provide real-world insights into tackling complex business challenges with the opportunity to interview for our internships + we will reward the first place team of two members with tickets to a Corona sponsored event!
The case competition is open to 2nd and 3rd year students completing their bachelor degree.
Event Details:
Date: Wednesday, October 29, 2024
Time: 9:00 AM – 4:00PM
Venue: Labatt Head Office, Toronto ON
Timeline:
September 27th @ 11:59PM EST Registration must be submitted by both team members
October 11th@ 11:59PM EST: In your team you must prepare a 6-10 slide presentation on how to solve the case
October 21st by 5PM EST: All successful candidates will be contacted to invite you to be a team finalist
October 29th from 9AM – 4PM: 10 Teams will be invited to join us at our Toronto Head office of Labatt Breweries of Canada to present their case LIVE to our panel of judges.
