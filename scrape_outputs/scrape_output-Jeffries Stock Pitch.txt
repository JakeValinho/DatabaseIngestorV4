Job description
Jefferies is proud to announce its second U.S. Equity Research Stock Pitch Competition.
This contest is ideal for undergraduate college sophomores with a finance-related major (i.e., Business, Accounting, Economics, or Mathematics) and/or a strong interest in Equity Research. By participating in this challenge, students will be able to demonstrate their market knowledge and interest in security valuation, as well as the potential to engage with Jefferies Equity Research professionals. Stock pitch winners will have the opportunity to interview for a 2026 Equity Research Summer Internship position.
Application Timeline
Submit your application by January 10, 2025
Finalists will be notified and provided additional details by January 17, 2025
Finalist will present their stock pitch at Jefferies New York headquarters on or around January 24, 2025

In order to participate, candidates must submit a two-page stock pitch which should include the company name, ticker, current price at time of submission, price target and respective upside/downside potential.

Stock Pitch Competition, Equity Research - New York
111
Jefferies is proud to announce its Inaugural Equity Research Stock Pitch Competition. This competition is ideal for sophomores with a finance related major (i.e. Business, Finance, Accounting, Economics,
or Mathematics) or alternatively a focus on professional writing. By participating in this competition, students will be able to demonstrate their market knowledge and interest in security valuation, as well as potentially engage with Equity Research professionals.
Stock Pitch
In order to participate, candidates must submit a two-page stock pitch which should include the company name, ticker, current price at time of submission, price target and respective upside/downside potential. Please upload your two-page stock pitch where it asks you to attach a personal statement as part of the application form. You are not required to upload a cover letter.
Additionally, we will be looking for:
- Overview of the Company
- Investment Thesis
- 1-3 Catalysts
- 1-2 Risks
- Valuation Analysis
Please be sure that the stock pitch only utilizes publicly available information.
Award
Stock pitch winners will:
- Receive a cash stipend of 1st: $500, 2nd: $400 and 3rd: $300
- Experience a day in the life of an Equity Research Analyst
- Present their stock pitch at Jefferies New York headquarters
- Have the opportunity to interview for a 2021 Equity Research Summer Internship position
This opportunity is closed to applications.
