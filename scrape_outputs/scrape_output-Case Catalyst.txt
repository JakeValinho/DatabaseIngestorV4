About us
We are an innovative platform dedicated to providing high school students with a unique opportunity to tackle real-world challenges faced by startups. Over the course of four weeks, students will work on cases developed in collaboration with our partner startups and compete with one another to find the best solution. Participants will benefit from mentorship sessions led by top university students from prestigious institutions such as Stanford, UChicago, Berkeley, Harvard, and UPenn, as well as seasoned industry professionals and consultants from leading firms such as Bain, McKinsey, and BCG. Our competition emphasizes experiential learning, allowing students to gain hands-on experience in problem-solving and business understanding. A standout feature of our competition is that the winners will have the exclusive opportunity to implement their solutions and intern with the startups, providing them with invaluable real-world experience. Entirely virtual, our competition welcomes students from all around the globe, breaking down geographical barriers and creating a diverse and inclusive learning environment. Through this competition, we aim to create the next generation of business leaders by equipping them with the skills and experiences necessary to succeed in a dynamic and globalized economy.

All high school students, no matter the country of residence, are eligible to sign up for this competition. This is a virtual case competition.
All students will be in teams of 3-4 students and will work together to solve the case
There are two ways a student can sign up:
As a Team - If you choose to sign up as a team, your team must have 3-4 members and each member is required to fill out the application form. In the application form, you will be asked to list your other team members and your team name.
As an Individual - If you do not have a team formed, you are still welcome to participate by registering individually for the competition. Once you have applied, we will create a team for you with other individual members and you will be introduced to them before the case launch day
The following are the dates you will need to be available to be able to participate in this case competition:
Case Launch Day - 20th July 2025
Two mentorship sessions and three workshops with case coaches between 20th July to 8th August 2025
