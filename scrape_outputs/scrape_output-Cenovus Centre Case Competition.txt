Cenovus Centre supply chain case competition
PLEASE NOTE: Registration for the competition has closed due to competition space being filled. If you’d like to be added to the competition waitlist, contact Karen Lacey, Manager, Cenovus Centre of Excellence in Sales and Supply Chain Management at
kmlacey@mun.ca
.
The inaugural Cenovus Centre of Excellence in Sales and Supply Chain Management Case Competition will be held Saturday, November 2, 2024, at the Memorial University Faculty of Business Administration.
This exciting new competition will provide an excellent opportunity for students to develop in-demand career skills, meet industry experts and win prizes!
You don’t need to be a supply chain management student already to benefit from this fantastic learning and networking opportunity. Are you interested in problem-solving, project management, sales or planning? Do you want to gain valuable experience in a team and connections to take into your career? Then this case competition is for you!
Who can participate?
This competition is open to any Memorial University undergraduate or graduate student. You must register as a team. Teams must be composed of four to six students total and a minimum of one must be a declared Business Administration student.
If you do not have a team, or are seeking additional members for a team, please feel free to reach out to Karen Lacey at
kmlacey@mun.ca
for information and assistance.
Why should you participate?
Earn cash prizes totalling $5,000 (Prizes for 1
st
, 2
nd
and 3
rd
place).
Develop in-demand career skills.
Meet industry experts and discover career options in supply chain management.
Gain experience with collaborating, problem-solving, networking and presenting in front of judges in a fun and supportive environment.
Learn more about Supply Chain Canada and opportunities at Memorial University.
Schedule and key details
Organizers anticipate a range of foundational skills and knowledge among participants. Thus, we will hold an optional information session and optional workshop prior to the competition, to help students better understand supply chain management and case studies, and support teams in preparation:
Optional information session
Thursday, Oct. 10, 5-7 p.m.
Faculty of Business Administration, RBC Atrium
Optional workshop
Monday, Oct. 28, 5-7 p.m.
Faculty of Business Administration, BN-3007
Case Competition Day
Saturday, Nov. 2
Competition:
9 a.m.- 4 p.m.
Faculty of Business Administration
Pitches and awards celebration:
6-9 p.m.
Faculty of Business Administration
Coffee break, lunch and awards celebration hot hors d’oeuvres will be provided, courtesy of our sponsors.

Another great student competition at @munbusiness! 

The Cenovus Centre of Excellence in Sales & Supply Chain Management held its inaugural supply chain case competition this weekend!
 
All 10 teams had great insights & professional, well considered pitches.

Congrats to all and hats off to the student teams who distinguished themselves in the top three: 
 
First place: Russell Noseworthy, Mary Ann Butler, Rebecca Jarvis, Abby Gruchy, Emma White, Tiernan MacCharles
 
Second place: Adithya Sudhan, Jay Vyas, Solina Mi, Anuj Nirwal, Pritaam Some
 
Third place: Fatemeh Kafrashi, Alisha Saxena, Pasan Navodaka, Amy White, Precious Okoro-Igwe
 
Thank you to the judges, Dr. Ginger Ke, Bonnie Donovan & Tony Williams. 

And to our generous sponsors, PF Collins International Trade Solutions, Energy NL, Crosbie, ExxonMobil, Supply Chain Canada & the Gardiner Centre. 
 
Thanks also to everyone behind the scenes who made this event possible!
