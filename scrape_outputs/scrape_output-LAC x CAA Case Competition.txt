About the event
On February 3, 2023 a memorandum of understanding was signed by the Presidents of Capilano University and Langara College.
In spirit of this, the Capilano Accounting Association (CAA) and the Langara Accounting Club (LAC) are incredibly honoured to host one of the first events that puts this memorandum into action through a case competition. This competition will replace each school's internal case competition, and there will be over $1,300 in available prizes.
Please join us on October 12th for our initial information session regarding what to expect, how to register, how to prepare, eligibility of you and your team, and a Q&A at the end.
CAA will hold their session from 11:30am-1:00pm on CapU's campus in room BR 203.
LAC will hold their session from 6:30pm-8:00pm on Langara's campus, room TBA.
Both session times will offer a virtual meeting.
Each session will provide the same information and students can attend either session regardless of which school they go to.
